DIRECTIONS

  • Autoresponder Address

    •     This is the e-mail address to which end users will
        send mail and receive the automated response.

  • Return Mail
        These parameters all deal with the mail being sent
        to the user, and how it will appear to them. The
        subject, address and body all pertain to the e-mail
        they will see in their inbox.

  • Valid Recipients

    •     Once the mail has been sent to your auto-responder
        by the end user, their original message can then be
        forwarded to up to two email addresses within your
        domain.

    Note: auto-responders operate on the same schedule as e-mail updates. Changes go into effect at one of four times during the day: 5am, 11am, 5pm and 11pm.
