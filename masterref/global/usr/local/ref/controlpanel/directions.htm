DIRECTIONS
  1. Choose a button if you are going to add an email address, change the destination of an email address, or completely delete an address.
  2. If you want to change an ALIAS, you must DELETE the alias, and add it back in the correct way.
  3. To add a selection type the alias in text box, and the destination in the text area below. Click Submit.
  4. To change the destination, select the column you wish to make a change on. The alias must remain exactly the same! Type in the new destination address. Click Submit.
  5. To delete an entire address and alias select the column you wish to delete. Click Submit.
  6. Changes will not be complete for a maximum of 6 hours. Changes go into effect at 5am, 11am, 5pm and 11pm. All times are relative to U.S. Central. If your submission is not complete by 24 hours, you should e-mail support.

NOTE: Be very careful in your selection, you can not undo once you click SUBMIT.
