Please note that some ISPs do not allow for customers to send mail via SMTP to servers outside of their network. If this is the case with your ISP, you may need to configure the "Outing mail (SMTP) server" as per their instructions. If after completing this walk through, you are unable to send mail, you may need to contact your ISP to find out what server you should use for your outgoing mail.
